Our dog walkers

A volunteer dog walker is a person who voluntarily offers their time and services to walk dogs. The goal of a volunteer dog walker is to provide the dogs with exercise, socialization, and human interaction to improve their physical and mental well-being while they wait to be adopted.

Our dog trainers

A dog trainer is a professional who specializes in teaching dogs obedience, behavior modification and other skills. They use positive reinforcement techniques to help dogs learn and develop good manners, and help pet owners address specific issues such as excessive barking, aggression, or separation anxiety.
